What I Do

Research at the intersection of theory & molecules

Two decades of work spanning ab-initio quantum chemistry, organic synthesis, and the noble-gas chemistry of exotic clusters.

🧪

Organic Chemistry

Regioselective C-H functionalization, halogenation, and the synthesis of macrocycles and heterocyclic scaffolds.

💻

Computational Chemistry

DFT and coupled-cluster studies, conceptual DFT, QSAR/QSTR modeling, and charge-transfer in DNA base pairs.

🌐

Noble-Gas & Cluster Chemistry

Probing noble-gas binding ability of metal oxides, cyanides, and the endohedral chemistry of cucurbiturils.
