About Dr. Gupta

Dr. Ashutosh Gupta is a Professor in the Department of Chemistry at Udai Pratap College, Varanasi, India, specializing in Organic Chemistry and Computational Chemistry.

He formerly served as Principal of Sahakari Post Graduate College, Mihrawan, Jaunpur (2021–23), and has held visiting research positions at leading institutions worldwide, collaborating with some of the most respected names in theoretical and computational chemistry.
